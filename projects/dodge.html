Dodge This!

Introduction

Dodge This! is a mini project that I coded while enroute a two hour plane ride. It was initially inspired by Ikaruga's unique color changing system where your ship cannot be hit by objects of the same color. I designed this game with nothing but a notebook and pencil and later implemented through javascript. Controls are (W,A,S,D) to move and left click to change color.

Movement and User Inputs

I used similar movement mechanics as in Shooter. User inputs are complex as in shooter, only needing the left mouse button to play the game.

Collision

Collision was also similar to shooter. However, there was a big difference, when objects bounce off each other, their trajectories change. Currently, changing the velocities is still a work in progress. Objects that overlap or collide in one frame will move a time step in the future with altered velocities so that they don't occupy similar spaces after bouncing.

Conclusion

This is an ongoing prototype. Features that I want to add are: correct implementation of bounce and a spacebar ability that teleports your player to a fixed distance away.
